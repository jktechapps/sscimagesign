❓ Frequently Asked Questions
What is the SSC CGL signature size in 2026? ▼
The SSC CGL 2026 signature must be JPEG/JPG format, file size between 10 KB and 20 KB, dimensions 6.0 cm (width) × 2.0 cm (height) at 300 DPI — which equals 709 × 236 pixels.
How do I resize my signature to 10KB–20KB? ▼
Simply upload your signature image above. This tool automatically resizes it to 709×236 pixels and compresses it to fit within 10–20 KB. Download the JPG and upload directly to ssc.gov.in.
Can I sign in blue ink for SSC CGL? ▼
No. SSC CGL 2026 requires black ink on plain white paper only. Blue ink signatures may be rejected during document verification.
Is my signature safe? Is it uploaded anywhere? ▼
Completely safe. All processing happens locally in your browser using JavaScript. Your image is never sent to any server and is not stored anywhere.
Why was my SSC signature rejected? ▼
Common reasons: file size outside 10–20 KB range, grey/dark background, signature in capital letters, blurred image, or wrong file format (non-JPEG). This tool fixes the size and format automatically.
Does this work on mobile? ▼
Yes. This tool works fully on Android and iPhone browsers. You can take a photo of your signature directly and upload it from your camera roll.
